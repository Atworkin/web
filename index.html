OUR OBJECTIVES

CARBON FOOTPRINT CALCULATION

Train students and staff ing in Carbon Footprint calculation

OUR MILESTONES:

-CF calculation reports. All the partner schools including students and staff have worked on the CF calculation and have suggested measures for emissions reduction

-Domestic CF calculation. Our daily activities and personal choices also cause emissions. How can we change our habits?

EMISSIONS REDUCTION

Reduce 30% of school emissions

OUR MILESTONES:

-Design of a carpooling app

-100% of renewable energies in the partner schools. Installation of solar panels

-Improving the energy efficiency of buildings

-Tree Plantations. The way to compensate our carbon emissions

GREEN SKILLS

Raise school-wide awareness on climate change and create a toolkit to reduce CF and environmental impact

OUR MILESTONES:

-Webinar on climate change

-Curricular activities into the classroom.

EXCHANGE OF GOOD PRACTICES
AND CULTURAL COMPETENCE

Get students to share good practices at an international level

OUR MILESTONES:

-International activities with students

SOCIAL INCLUSION AND GENDER EQUALITY

Involve 100% of students with special needs in the project

INVOLVING COMPANIES/SCHOOLS
IN SUSTAINABILITY ISSUES

Involve 100% partners companies in CF calculation and reduction issues through students internship projects and coach and support other schools in the calculation of CO2 emissions

OUR MILESTONES:

-Collaboration with partner companies and schools: Energy audits and analysis carried out by students

DESIGN OF SUSTAINABILITY/ENVIRONMENTAL
POLICIES IN EACH SCHOOL

Design school environmental policies and sustainability plans

OUR MILESTONES:

-International Environmental Statement. Commemorating the International Earth Day, the six partners of the project sign an Environmental Statement

-Workshops about Environmental Management Systems, Corporate Social Responsibility and Sustainable Development Goals
